  • Scorch
    Bulky Ogre-class titan focused around powerful thermite-based area-denial abilities that allow it to do deal heavy damage to any titan that is in the wrong position.

    Health: 12500 hp (4 bars)

    Dashes: 1 (faster cooldown with Turbo Engine)

  • Weapon
    Equiped with a T-203 Thermite Launcher that is a large flare gun that shoots canisters of thermite that explode on impact with the environment aswell as any target.
    Deals heavy damage towards enemy titans aswell as instantly killing pilots.

    Capacity: 1 round

  • Abilities
    Tactical: Incendiary Trap

    On use:
    Launches an incendiary trap that explodes when ignited

    Ordnance: Firewall

    On use:
    Launches a lane of fire in the direction scorch is looking.

    Defensive: Flame Shield

    On use:
    Shoots fire ahead of scorch which consumes any projectiles it catches aswell as dealing heavy damage towards enemy titans and other enemies it touches.

    Core: Flame Core

    On use:
    Launches a wave of flames in the direction scorch is looking.

  • Kits
    Wildfire Launcher:
    Enhances the damage from the T-203 Thermite Launcher.

    Tempered Plating:
    Scorch no longer takes damage from its own thermite.

    Inferno Shield:
    Flame shield lasts longer and absorbs more damage while dealing more damage aswell.

    Fuel for the Fire:
    Firewall's cooldown is reduced by 2 seconds.

    Scorched Earth:
    Flame Core ignites the ground, leaving thermite in it's wake.
