• The Titans of Titanfall 2

    Developed by Respawn entertainment, Titanfall 2 is a fast-paced, first person, movement shooter with "Titans" as a heavily present game mechanic

    Titans are equiped with "Dashes" that offer enhanced horizontal movement capabilities aswell as an "ordnance", "tactical" and "defensive" ability aswell as an ultimate "Core".

  • ION
    Well rounded Atlas-class titan focused around energy management for a balanced titan suitable for any range.

    Health: 10000 hp (4 bars)
    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: Splitter Rifle
    Tactical: Tripwire
    Ordnance: Laser Shot
    Defensive: Vortex Shield
    Core: Laser Core
  • Tone
    Well rounded Atlas-class titan focused around sonar abilities that synergize with tracking rockets that allow for multiple ways of engaging.

    Health: 10000 hp (4 bars)
    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: 40mm Tracker Cannon
    Tactical: Sonar Pulse
    Ordnance: Tracking Rockets
    Defensive: Particle Wall
    Core: Salvo Core
  • Northstar
    Nimble Stryder-class titan focused around high long range burst damage with its plasma railgun and area denial using missiles.

    Health: 7500 hp (3 bars)
    Dashes: 2 (3 with Turbo engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: Plasma Railgun
    Tactical: VTOL-Hover
    Ordnance: Cluster Missile
    Defensive: Tether Trap
    Core: Flight Core
  • Ronin
    Nimble Stryder-class titan focused around hit-and-run tactics with a close range shotgun aswell as a sword for higher melee damage and damage mitigation.

    Health: 7500 hp (3 bars)
    Dashes: 2 (3 with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: Leadwall Shotgun
    Tactical: Phase Dash
    Ordnance: Arc Wave
    Defensive: Sword Block
    Core: Sword Core
  • Legion
    Bulky Ogre-class titan focused around sustained fire with its Predator Cannon that offer alternative fire modes for both long range and short range burst damage.

    Health: 12500 hp (5 bars)
    Dashes: 1 (faster cooldown with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: Predator Cannon
    Tactical: Mode Switch
    Ordnance: Power Shot
    Defensive: Gun Shield
    Core: Smart Core
  • Scorch
    Bulky Ogre-class titan focused around powerful thermite-based area-denial abilities that allow it to do deal heavy damage to any titan that is in the wrong position.

    Health: 12500 hp (5 bars)
    Dashes: 1 (faster cooldown with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: T-203 Thermite Launcher
    Tactical: Incendiary Trap
    Ordnance: Firewall
    Defensive: Flame Shield
    Core: Flame Core
  • Monarch
    Well rounded semi-Vanguard-class titan focused around acquiring upgrades that improves survivability and offensive capabilities and makes it a powerhouse on the battlefield once fully upgraded.

    Health: 10000 hp (4 bars)
    Health With Superior Chassis Upgrade: 15000 (6 bars)
    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

    Abilities:

    Weapon: XO-16 Chaingun
    Tactical: Rearm
    Ordnance: Rocket barrage
    Defensive: Energy Siphon
    Core: Upgrade Core
