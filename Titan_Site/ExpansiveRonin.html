  • Ronin
    Nimble Stryder-class titan focused around hit-and-run tactics with a close range shotgun aswell as a sword for higher melee damage and damage mitigation.

    Health: 7500 hp (3 bars)
  • Weapon
    Equipped with a Leadwall which is a powerful triple barreled titan carried shotgun that shoots shots that are capable of dealing heavy damage to titans aswell as killing pilots very efficiently.

    Capacity: 4 rounds

  • Abilities
    Tactical: Phase Dash

    On use:
    Teleports into a parallel dimension while dashing forward and exiting a few seconds after

    Ordnance: Arc Wave

    On use:
    Ronin launches a wave that travels forward and deals noticeable damage towards pilots and titans while slowing them down and disabling defensive equipment.

    Defensive: Sword Block.

    On use:
    Reduces incoming damage by 75% when channeling.

    Core: Sword Core

    On use:

    Enhances abilities:

    Dash recharges significantly faster.
    Sword Block now negates 90% of incoming damage.
    Broadsword now deals increased damage.

  • Kits
    Ricochet Rounds:
    The Leadwall's rounds bounce off surfaces.

    Thunderstorm:
    Arc wave has 2 charges

    Temporal Anomaly:
    Phase Dash is available more often.

    Highlander:
    Titan kills extend the duration of Sword Core.

    Phase Reflex:
    When doomed, Ronin phases out of danger.
