  • Monarch
    Well rounded semi-Vanguard-class titan focused around acquiring upgrades that improves survivability and offensive capabilities and makes it a powerhouse on the battlefield once fully upgraded.

    Health: 10000 hp (3 bars)

    Health With Superior Chassis Upgrade: 15000 (6 bars)

    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

  • Weapon
    Equipped with a XO-16 chaingun that deals moderate damage and medium damage towards pilots at a high fire rate.

    Can be upgraded with Arc Rounds that drain enemy shields aswell as XO-16 accelerator upgrade that increases damage output aswell as increasing fire rate but forces the gun to increase in fire rate from a slow start.

    Capacity: 40 (50 with Arc Rounds)
  • Abilities
    Tactical: Rearm

    On use:
    Resets cooldowns of Dashes, Ordnance and Defensive ability.

    Ordnance: Rocket Salvo

    On use:
    Fires a barrage of rockets that travel in a straight direction.

    Defensive: Energy Siphon

    On use:
    Drains shield from enemies it hits while giving Monarch shield in return.

    Core: Upgrade Core

    On use:
    Upgrades Monarch according to the chosen upgrade path.

  • Kits
    Shield Amplifier:
    Energy siphon's shield gain is increased by 35%

    Ebergy Thief:
    Charges Monarch's Core 10% faster and performing an execution on enemy titans causes you to steal their battery aswell.

    Rapid Rearm:
    Reduces the Cooldown of Rearm by 5 seconds.

    Survival of the Fittest
    Batteries can repair Monarch out of doomed state.
