  • ION
    Well rounded Atlas-class titan focused around energy management for a balanced titan suitable for any range.

    Health: 10000 hp (4 bars)

    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

  • Weapon
    Equiped with a Splitter Rifle, a medium range, energy-based weapon that fires slow moving energy particles that are effective against pilots aswell as other Titans.

    The Splitter Rifle is capable of switching fire mode to one that shoots out waves of projectiles that allow it to deal higher damage up close at the cost of draining from ION's energy gauge.

    Capacity: 40 Rounds

  • Abilities
    Tactical: Tripwire

    On use:
    Fires out 3 highly explosive mines in a fan-like pattern in front of ION, consumes some of ION's energy, consumes 0 energy if Zero-Point Tripwire is equipped.

    When Triggered:
    Causes an explosion in the nearby vicinity that instantly kills human-sized targets aswell as dealing heavy damage towards titans.

    Ordnance: Laser Shot

    On use:
    Fires a powerful laser shot at the cost of about 60% of ION's energy, that deals Heavy damage towards titans aswell as killing human-sized targets instantly.

    Defensive: Vortex Shield

    On use:
    Projects a Vortex Shield that is capable of catching non-energy-based projectiles and storing them in the shield to throw them back in the viewing direction to deal damage depending on how many and what types of projectile the shield catches.

    Core: Laser Core

    On use:
    Charges briefly before unleashing a powerful beam of energy that deals continous heavy damage towards enemy titans aswell as killing any human sized target extremely fast.

  • Kits
    Entangled energy:
    Splitter Rifle critical hits restore energy.

    Zero-Point Tripwire:
    Tripwire deployment uses zero energy.

    Vortex Amplifier:
    Increases Vortex Shield's return damage output by 35%.

    Grand Cannon:
    Laser Core lasts longer.

    Refraction Lens:
    Splitter rifle alternative fire splits 5 ways instead of 3
