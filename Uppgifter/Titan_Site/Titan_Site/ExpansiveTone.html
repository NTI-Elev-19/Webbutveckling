  • Tone

  • Tone
    Well rounded Atlas-class titan focused around sonar abilities that synergize with tracking rockets that allow for multiple ways of engaging.

    Health: 10000 hp (4 bars)

    Dashes: 1 (2 with Turbo Engine)

  • Weapon
    Equiped with a 40mm Cannon, a semi-automatic, tracker cannon that shoots 40mm shells that allow tone to increase it's "locks".

    Deals medium damage towards titans and kill pilots instantly upon direct hits.

    The 40mm cannon is capable of charging up 3 round bursts with the Burst loader kit.

    Capacity: 12

  • Abilities
    Tactical: Sonar Pulse

    On use:
    Fires out a projectile that reveals enemies near it aswell as giving a sonar lock to enemy titans and reapers that enter its vicinity.

    Ordnance: Tracker Rockets

    On use:
    Fires a salvo of tracker rockets that follow any target Tone has with 3 sonar locks on it.

    Defensive: Particle Wall

    On use:
    Projects a stationary wall that blocks any projectiles coming from the front while allowing projectiles to pass from the rear.

    Core: Salvo Core

    On use:
    Releases a barrage of rockets that follow the direction Tone is Looking.

  • Kits
    Enhanced Tracker Rounds:
    Critical hits apply 2 sonar locks on targets instead of 1.

    Reinforced Particle Wall:
    Particle Wall lasts longer and blocks more damage.

    Pulse-Echo:
    After a short delay, Sonar Pulse echoes a second pulse applying sonar locks to targets again.

    Rocket Barrage:
    Tracker Rockets Fire 2 additional missiles.

    Burst Loader:
    Aiming allows the 40mm tracker cannon to store up to 3 shots for burst fire.
