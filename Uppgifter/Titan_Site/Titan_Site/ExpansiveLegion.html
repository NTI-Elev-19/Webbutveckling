  • Legion
    Bulky Ogre-class titan focused around sustained fire with its Predator Cannon that offer alternative fire modes for both long range and short range burst damage.

    Health: 12500 hp (4 bars)

    Dashes: 1 (faster cooldown with Turbo Engine)

  • Weapon
    Equipped with a Predator Cannon that is a large rotary cannon that is capable of switching between a long range mode that consumes more ammunition aswell as a short range mode that has a lower accuracy.
    Legion is capable of revving up its gun to unleash a burst of bullets that deal heavy damage towards titans aswell as killing pilots with ease.

    Capacity: 100 rounds

  • Abilities
    Tactical: Mode Switch

    On use:
    Switches the Predator Cannon from long range to short range or vice versa.

    Ordnance: Power Shot

    On use:
    Revs up the Predator Cannon to shoot a powerful burst of bullets that changes shape depending on the active mode.

    Defensive: Gun Shield

    On use:
    Projects a shield that is locked to the Predator Cannon.

    Core: Smart Core

    On use:
    Predator Cannon automatically aims at any target it sees.

  • Kits
    Enhanced Ammo Capacity:
    Increases the ammo capacity of the Predator Cannon from 100 to 140 rounds.

    Sensor Array:
    Smart Core lasts longer.

    Bulwark:
    Gun shield blocks twice as much damage.

    Light-Weight Alloys:
    Move faster while the Predator Cannon is spun up.

    Hidden Compartment:
    Power Shot has 1 additional charge, but deals 15% less damage.
