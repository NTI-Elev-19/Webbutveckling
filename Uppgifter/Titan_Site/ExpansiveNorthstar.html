  • Northstar

  • Northstar
    Nimble Stryder-class titan focused around high long range burst damage with its plasma railgun and area denial using missiles.


    Health: 7500 hp (3 bars)
  • Weapon
    Equipped with a Plasma Railgun, a powerful titan carried railgun that can charge up to increase power in its shot to deal significantly higher damage.

    Capable of killing pilots in a single hit without charging

    Capacity: 6 Rounds

  • Abilities
    Tactical: VTOL-Hover

    On use:
    Launches into the air and begins to hover in the air. Air movement can be enhanced with Viper-Thrusters.

    Ordnance: Cluster Missile

    On use:
    Fires a missile that explodes and unleashes a hail of smaller explosives in the nearby area.

    Defensive: Tether Trap

    On use:
    Launches a Tether Trap that attaches to enemy titans and reapers, can be disabled by shooting at it or it will detach after a short period of time.

    Core: Flight Core

    On use:
    Launches into the air and begins to hover while repeatedly shooting rockets.

  • Kits
    Piercing Shot:
    Plasma Railgun rounds fire through targets.

    Enhanced Payload:
    Cluster Missile's secondary explosions last for longer and cover a larger area.

    Twin Traps:
    Tether Traps launch 2 traps instead of 1.

    Viper Thrusters:
    Move Faster when using VTOL-Hover and Flight Core.

    Threat Optics:
    Enemies are Highlighted when zooming in.
